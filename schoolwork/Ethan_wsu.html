Facts about wsu

  1. WSU delivered $406.7 million in financial aid and scholarships for undergraduate students (2018-19). In the 2018-19 academic year, 76% of WSU undergraduates received some form of financial aid.
  2. WSU went though sevreal name changes. From Weber Stake Academy to Weber Normal College. Yes, you read that right, normal. I had a hard time believing that It had the word normal in our name, but it was typical for teacher colleges at the time.
  3. WSU's original name was Washington State Agricultural College and School of Sciences
  4. The mascot Butch T. Cougar was first seen in 1927.
  5. In 1993, WSU Vancouver was one of the first institutions in the country to use interactive electronic classrooms. (classes via video]
